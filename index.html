BITEX (BIT)

BITEX is an ERC-20 utility token deployed on the Polygon PoS network. It is designed for fast, low-cost transactions, digital payments, DeFi applications, and trading on decentralized and centralized exchanges (DEX/CEX). BITEX aims to provide a simple and efficient blockchain-based financial tool for everyday crypto users and online payment integrations.

About BITEX

BITEX is a blockchain-based project focused on providing a practical utility token for payment solutions, DeFi services, and digital asset transactions. By operating on Polygon, BITEX benefits from high transaction speed and minimal fees. The project is community-driven and aims to expand real-world crypto payment adoption.
